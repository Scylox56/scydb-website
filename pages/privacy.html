Introduction

Welcome to ScyDB. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our movie database service.

By using ScyDB, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and profile photo
  • Authentication: Securely hashed passwords
  • Preferences: Watchlist, reviews, and personalization settings

Usage Information

  • Activity: Movies viewed, searches performed, and reviews written
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Analytics: Page views and feature usage (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

Account Management

Create and maintain your user account, authenticate logins, and provide customer support.

Personalization

Customize your experience with personalized recommendations and saved preferences.

Security

Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to our services.

Analytics

Improve our services through usage analysis and performance monitoring.

Data Protection & Security

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.

Secure Storage

Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access and regular backups.

Privacy by Design

We collect only necessary data and anonymize analytics whenever possible.

Your Rights

Access Your Data

Request a copy of all personal data we have about you.

Correct Your Data

Update or correct any inaccurate personal information.

Delete Your Data

Request deletion of your account and associated data.

Opt-Out

Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
