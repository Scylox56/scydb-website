About ScyDB

The most comprehensive movie database for true cinephiles.

Our Mission

To turn movie obsession into something slightly productive. ScyDB exists because one dev decided, “Why just watch movies when you can also build an entire platform around them?” With reviews you can actually trust (because they weren’t written by bots), personalized picks that might know your taste, and just enough sarcasm to keep things interesting—we’re here to make movie discovery feel less like a chore and more like a group chat recommendation that went too far.

The Mastermind

ScyDB was launched in 2025 by one movie-obsessed developer who thought, ‘Yeah, I can build that.’ Several sleepless nights, hundreds of console logs, and too many rewatches later, here we are. Whether you're hunting for hidden gems or judging movies by their posters (we all do it), ScyDB is here to make movie discovery way more fun—and way less corporate.
